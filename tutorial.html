HTML5 Semantic Elements

Semantics is the study of the meanings of words and phrases in language.

Semantic elements are elements with a meaning.

What are Semantic Elements?

A semantic element clealry describes its meaning to both the browser and the developer

Examples of non-semantic elements: div and span - Tells nothing about its content.

Examples of semantic elemets: form, table and img - Clearly defines its conent.
